Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

In short: STUD'IT Notify respects your privacy. We only collect data strictly necessary for the app to function. Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

1. Data Controller Identity and Contact Information

DATA MKEY

Developer and publisher of the STUD'IT Notify application

Email: support@datamkey.com

Website: www.datamkey.com

2. Data Collected and Purposes

2.1. Authentication Data

Data collected:

  • Username
  • Password (encrypted via Keycloak)
  • Email address
  • Access roles and permissions

Purpose: Secure authentication and access control to the application.

Legal basis: Contract performance (application access).

Retention period: As long as your account is active, then deletion within 30 days of deactivation.

2.2. Contacts Access

Data collected:

  • Phone numbers of contacts stored in your device
  • First and last names associated with contacts

Purpose: Automatic retrieval of confirmed students' phone numbers for sending SMS reminders.

Legal basis: Explicit consent requested on first access.

Important:

  • Phone numbers are NEVER stored on our servers
  • Access is read-only and local to your device
  • Numbers are only used at the time of sending SMS
  • You can revoke access at any time in iOS Settings (Settings > STUD'IT Notify > Contacts)

2.3. Event Data

Data accessed:

  • Event title and description
  • Dates, times, and locations
  • List of registered and confirmed students
  • Confirmation statuses

Purpose: Display events and manage SMS notifications.

Legal basis: Contract performance (event management).

Storage: This data is stored on our servers and accessed via secure API.

2.4. Technical Data

Data collected locally:

  • Authentication token (securely stored via iOS Keychain)
  • Custom message template preferences
  • Splash screen status (first launch)

Purpose: Technical app functionality and user experience improvement.

Storage: Only on your device, never transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure authentication: Via Keycloak with OAuth2 protocol
  • Communication encryption: All communications with our servers use HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure local storage: Tokens are stored in iOS Keychain (system vault)
  • Access control: Role and permission system (only users with "app-sms-users" role can access)
  • No phone number storage: Phone numbers are never transmitted or stored on our servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

The only exceptions are:

  • Internal infrastructure: For authentication and event access (DATA MKEY servers)
  • SMS operator: When sending an SMS, numbers are transmitted to your mobile operator (iOS system functionality)
  • Legal obligations: If required by a competent authority in legal proceedings

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing: Limit the use of your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent at any time (e.g., contacts access)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@datamkey.com

Response time: We commit to responding within 30 days.

6. Data Transfers Outside EU

No data transfer outside the European Union.

All your data is stored and processed exclusively on servers located in Belgium/European Union.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This application does not use any cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics tools.

We do not track your activity and do not collect any browsing or usage data for statistical or marketing purposes.

8. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following services:

  • Keycloak (self-hosted): Authentication and identity management (DATA MKEY servers)
  • Backend API (self-hosted): Event data access (DATA MKEY servers)
  • Expo (Expo.dev): Mobile development framework (used only for building, no user data transmitted)

No third-party service has access to your personal data.

9. Minors

This application is intended for event organizers (adults). It does not knowingly collect personal data from minors under 16 years of age.
